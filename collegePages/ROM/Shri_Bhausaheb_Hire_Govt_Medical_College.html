About

Shri Bhausaheb Hire Government Medical College (SBHGMC) is a medical institution in Dhule, Maharashtra. It is affiliated to the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences and recognised by the Medical Council of India.[1] It was established in 1989.

SBHGMC principally offers a graduate degree in medicine namely MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine Bachelor of Surgery). It has a total student intake capacity of 100

Students come from all over India. The division of seats for students from the state of Maharashtra and Rest of India is as follows:

  • Students from MHTCET (Maharashtra Health Sciences Common Entrance test): 85%
  • Students from PMT (All India Pre Medical Test): 15%

The hospital attached to the college is Government Hospital, Dhule, also known as Civil Hospital, Dhule. The Civil Hospital is in Dhule city and is a major hospital in Dhule district.
