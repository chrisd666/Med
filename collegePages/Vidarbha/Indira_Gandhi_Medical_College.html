About

Indira Gandhi Government Medical College & Hospital (IGGMCH) (also known as Mayo Hospital) established in the year 1968 is located in Nagpur near the Nagpur railway station , run by the Government of Maharashtra. It is one of the two Government Medical Colleges in Nagpur city and one among the top ten Govt. Medical Colleges in Maharashtra.

Indira Gandhi Government Medical College & Hospital has Medical Council of India recognition for 150 MBBS admissions. Postgraduate courses are available in the subjects, Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Pathology, Microbiology, Forensic Medicine, Community Medicine, Ophthalmology, ENT, Orthopedics, Anesthesiology, Paediatrics, General Medicine, General Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Radio Diagnosis, Pulmonary Medicine, Orthopaedics.
