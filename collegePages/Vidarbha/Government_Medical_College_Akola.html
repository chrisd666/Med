About

Government Medical College, Akola is one of the high points of the Akola city. The attached teaching hospital - Survopchar Rugnalaya (Multispeciality hospital) was previously known as Main Hospital. It is situated near the Ashok Vaatika square.

It has an intake capacity of 150 students each year. Mode of admission is through Maharashtra state level competitive examination 'MHT-CET'.
